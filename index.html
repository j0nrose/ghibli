About Studio Ghibli

Studio Ghibli is a Japanese animation film studio headquartered in Tokyo. The studio is best known for its animated feature films, many of which have been internationally distributed.

Founded in 1985 by directors Hayao Miyazaki and Isao Takahata, Studio Ghibli has created some of the most beloved animated films in cinema history, known for their environmental themes, strong characters, and breathtaking hand-drawn animation.

Our films have touched the hearts of audiences worldwide, earning numerous awards including Academy Awards, and continuing to inspire new generations of storytellers and dreamers.
